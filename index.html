Our recipe so you can try at home

  • Total cook time: 40 minutes 10 seconds
  • Prep time: 10 minutes 10 seconds
  • Cook time: 30 minutes
  • Recipe servings: 2

Ingredints

  1. 1/2 an iceberg lettuce, shredded (refrigerated)
  2. 2-3 Tbsp sour cream
  3. 2 jalapenos, sliced
  4. 2 green chilies, sliced
  5. 1 tsp tomato puree
  6. 1 red onion
  7. 1/3 red, yellow and green bell pepper
  8. 100 gm cottage cheese (paneer)
  9. 150 gm rajma (kidney beans), cooked
  10. 1.5 Tbsp olive oil
  11. 2-3 cloves bashed garlic
  12. 1 tsp chilly powder
  13. 1 tsp cumin powder
  14. 2 green tomatoes
  15. 1.5 tsp black pepper
  16. Juice of 1 lemon
  17. 2 Tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  18. 50 gm grated cheddar
  19. Taco shells

Process

Heat Ingredints
- In a pan, heat olive oil, add the bell peppers, onions, garlic, green chilies and saute nicely.
Add rajma
- Add the cooked kidney beans to this mixture.
Say cheese
- Crumble the cottage cheese into the pan.
It's chilly here
- Add the cumin and chilly powder.
Mix-it
- Mix all the ingredients together well.
La tomatina
- Add 1 teaspoon of tomato puree to the mix.
Season it
- Season with salt and pepper, add lime juice and chopped coriander and mix well.
Mix all
- In a mix add 2 green tomatoes, 1.5 teaspoon olive oil, a big pinch of salt and a small bunch of coriander leaves to make a nice green salsa.
Warmth
- Warm the taco shells in a moderate oven 170Â°C for 5 to 6 minutes
Final touch
- Start with adding the shredded lettuce in the taco, and then add the kidney beans and cottage cheese mixture, jalapenos, cream cheese and grated cheese with the green tomatillo salsa.
